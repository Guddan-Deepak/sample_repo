Darjeeling

About

Darjeeling, is a city in the northernmost region of the Indian state of West Bengal. Located in the Eastern Himalayas, it has an average elevation of 2,045 metres (6,709 ft).

Tea Plantations

Tea and Darjeeling are synonymous. Darjeeling Tea is world renowned for its flavour, which is unequaled by other tea producing areas not only of India but also of the world. Darjeeling still manufactures the tea by the original methods known as the 'orthodox' method.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Darjeeling is between March to May (spring-summer) and September to November (autumn), when the weather is pleasant, and the skies are clear, offering the best views of the mountains.

Key Atrractions

Key attractions in Darjeeling include the famous Tiger Hill, known for its stunning sunrise views over Mount Kanchenjunga, the Darjeeling Zoo, and the Batasia Loop. The Darjeeling Tea Estates also offer scenic views and tours of tea production facilities.

Tiger Hill:This vantage point is famous for its spectacular sunrise views over the Himalayas, including a breathtaking glimpse of Mount Everest on clear days. Watching the sun's first rays illuminate the peaks of Kanchenjunga is an unforgettable experience.

Batasia Loop :This engineering marvel is a spiral railway track near Ghoom that allows the Toy Train to descend the hill. It offers magnificent views of the surrounding valleys and the snow-covered peaks.

Handicrafts

Handicrafts of Darjeeling are well known, for it being a famous tourist spot. Skillful wood/bamboo work, metal work/artefacts, jewellery, paintings, carpets and woollen garments run unparalleled. These unique handicrafts resemble the traditional imprints of Tibetian, Gorkha and Lepcha cultures.

Metal Crafts by Gorkhali Artisans, Non-metal Crafts by Gorkhali Artisans, Traditional Handicrafts of the Tibetan Artisans, Handicrafts made by the Lepcha Artisans are skilled workers here.

Woolen Items, Brass & Bell Metal, Wood Carving, Cane & Bamboo, White Metal Jewellery, Painting (on cloth), Embroidery are done here in Darjeeling.
